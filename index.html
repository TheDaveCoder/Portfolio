About

My passion for tech began in 8th grade, inspired by an ICT professor whose enthusiasm for programming sparked a curiosity in me that I’ve pursued ever since. Starting with basics in C++, Python, and Ruby, I quickly realized this field was for me. By 11th grade, after diving into a range of topics and even placing in city-wide web development contests, I’d found my path: Full-stack development and DevOps.

Now a college student, I’m building on those early foundations, gaining experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and exploring the world of cloud solutions. I thrive on creating seamless, user-focused designs and crafting systems that blend functionality with efficiency. Driven by a love for learning and solving complex problems, I’m excited to bring innovative, practical solutions to the tech industry as I work toward becoming a skilled full-stack developer and DevOps engineer.
